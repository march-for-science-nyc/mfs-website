Fireside Chat

During this time when we are all staying home for the greater good, science still goes on. To be able to continue to discuss how it's received and impacts the world, we've transformed our monthly happy hours into bi-weekly Fireside Chats, held over Zoom. Come chat with us! This event series also includes monthly BIPOC spaces.

STEM the Vote

STEM the Vote is a Get Out the Vote initiative in partnership with Plus 1 Vote and Science Rising. We seek to (1) increase voter turnout, focusing on communities with historically low political incorporation and (2) emphasize the need to elect public officials committed to science-based policies grounded in social justice.
